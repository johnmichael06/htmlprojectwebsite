Income Tax Calculator

Income Tax Calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to swiftly determine the income tax liability for individuals based on their taxable income. By entering the taxable income in Philippine pesos (PHP), the calculator instantly computes the corresponding income tax amount using predefined tax brackets and rates as per the Philippine tax system. This efficient and accessible tool assists individuals in estimating their income tax obligations, facilitating better financial planning and decision-making.
